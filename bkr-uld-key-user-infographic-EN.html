UBR Tracking
🚫 Problem 1: Invisible to Capacity
Set pallet requests are NOT visible to capacity teams in COMIS because there's no AWB or UBR number. Capacity manually evaluates based on availability.
🚫 Problem 2: Accounting Gap
Equipment creates cost flow but since it has no actual counterpart, costs can't be reflected to agents. This creates accounting issues.
🚫 Problem 3: Dual Entry
Data must be entered separately in 3CS and OPR344. No integration means manual work and potential inconsistencies.
