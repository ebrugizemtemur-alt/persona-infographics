Cargo Marketing & Sales Solutions Directorate • Operational User • Stock & Messaging Management

✓ Manages AWB stock lifecycle from IATA allocation to purge recovery across HQ, Regions, and Agents.

PERSONA SNAPSHOT
👤 IDENTITY
📋
Kevser / Burak
AWB Stock Administrator / Key User
🎯 Primary Role
Manages AWB stock allocation from HQ to Regions to Agents, monitors purge process, handles messaging configurations
🔍 Focus Area
Stock distribution hierarchy, purge job monitoring, messaging protocols (FWB/FHL/FSU/FFM), IATA compliance
⏱️ Work Pattern
Daily purge monitoring, stock requests via email, Excel exports for reporting. Heavy workload during agent onboarding.
🔗 Connected Teams
Regional Offices Station Personnel Agents (IATA) Customer Care Accounting
🎯 ROLE & RESPONSIBILITIES
📋 Main Responsibility
Ensure continuous AWB stock availability across the network while maximizing reuse through timely purge cycles
✓ Success Criteria
✓ No stock-out situations at any station/agent
✓ Purge jobs running without interruption
✓ AWB reuse cycle maintained (currently 4 years)
✓ Messaging (FWB/FSU) errors minimized
🏷️ Main Blockers
Purge job stops → 40K AWB accumulation No automatic stock withdrawal from inactive agents Excel-based reporting and requests No customs rule validation at assignment

🎯 Be proactively notified of issues, not discover them manually

PAIN POINT → SOLUTION → PRIORITIZATION MATRIX
Pain Point Root Cause Proposed Solution Expected Outcome Severity Priority
Purge job stops silently — 40K AWBs accumulate, stock assignment halts No monitoring/alerting for background jobs Job status dashboard + automatic alerts when job fails Proactive issue detection, no operational disruption CRITICAL P1
No automatic stock withdrawal — Inactive agents hold unused stock indefinitely No business rule for automatic reclaim Auto-reclaim unused stock after 1 year + notification Stock utilization improved, no manual tracking HIGH P1
No customs validation at assignment — China has 10-year rule, can't check Route unknown at assignment time Validate at reservation time instead, with country rules Customs penalties avoided HIGH P2
Excel-based reporting — STK007 lacks origin-dest, must export Report not designed for this use case Add origin-dest column + purged AWB column to STK007 No Excel dependency, faster decisions MEDIUM P2
Minimum stock threshold not enforced — Can assign below safe level No parametric control Block assignment if below threshold + warning Stock-out prevention MEDIUM P2
4-year purge cycle too long — Stock tied up unnecessarily Accounting processes can take years Decouple accounting from purge, reduce to 3 years Faster stock turnover MEDIUM P3
