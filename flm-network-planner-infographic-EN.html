1
PERSONA SNAPSHOT
🌐
Network Planner
Kargo Network Planlama Müdürlüğü
PRIMARY GOAL
Design seasonal flight network, coordinate slot conferences, evaluate regional route requests, and optimize cargo route profitability
KEY USERS
Sena Ekinay, Bahadır Bozatlı (Network Planning)
DEPARTMENT
Kargo Pazarlama ve Satış Çözümleri Direktörlüğü - Network Planlama Md.
PLANNING CYCLES
☀️ Summer Season (IATA) ❄️ Winter Season (IATA)
2
JOBS TO BE DONE
📅 Seasonal Flight Network Design
Create draft seasonal schedule before slot conference. Design cargo flight network based on market analysis, regional demand, and feasibility studies.
🎫 Slot Conference Coordination
Prepare draft schedule for IATA slot conferences. Coordinate slot approvals with airports and authorities. Revise schedule after conference feedback.
📊 Regional Request Evaluation
Collect route requests via email from regional chiefs. Evaluate feasibility for both new and existing routes. Assess market potential and profitability.
🤝 Cross-Team Coordination
Share draft plan with Operations and Sales teams. Consider airport congestion and time slot constraints. Incorporate feedback and make mutual revisions.
🚀 Season Plan Launch
Open season plan for sale 45 days before season starts. Coordinate with COMIS and Netline systems for consistent data. Get upper management approvals.

PAIN POINTS MATRIX
Pain Point Description Business Impact Severity
No Reverse Netline-COMIS Integration No integration from COMIS back to Netline. Changes in COMIS don't reflect automatically Manual sync required; data inconsistencies between systems CRITICAL
Email-Based Request Collection Regional route requests collected via email without structured system Requests can be missed; difficult to track and prioritize HIGH
Excel-Based Feasibility Analysis Flight profitability calculated in Excel; not integrated with live data Manual work; outdated data; calculation errors possible HIGH
Capacity Not Visible in Netline Flight capacity managed by another department; not visible in Netline Network planners can't see capacity impact of their decisions MEDIUM
45-Day Sales Opening Constraint Season must be opened for sale 45 days in advance; tight timeline for coordination Rushed approvals; limited time for revision MEDIUM

KEY CONCEPTS
SLOT CONFERENCE
IATA-organized meeting where airlines negotiate and secure airport slots for seasonal schedules
SS FILE
Standard Schedule file format used to communicate finalized schedule to other teams
5TH FREEDOM
Traffic right allowing airline to carry cargo between two foreign countries (affects route feasibility)
GANTT CHART
Visual timeline in Netline showing flight sequences and aircraft utilization
COLLABORATION
Schedule Planning Team Sales Teams Operations Regional Chiefs
