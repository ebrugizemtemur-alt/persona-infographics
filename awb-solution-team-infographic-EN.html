PURGE JOB ARCHITECTURE
⚙️ BACKGROUND JOBS
🔍
JOB 1: Mark for Purge
Scans AWBs, checks 4-year rule (Sales + Operations + Accounting complete), marks eligible ones
↓
📤
JOB 2: Transfer to HQ
Transfers marked AWBs to HQ pool at 16:00 (batch for performance)
🚨 CRITICAL PROBLEM
If jobs stop for 10 days, approximately 40,000 AWBs accumulate in limbo. Stock assignment halts completely. No automatic alert exists — discovered manually by Key Users.
📋 PURGE CRITERIA
✅ Sales Complete
Reservation closed, no pending updates
✅ Operations Complete
Cargo flown and delivered (DLV status)
✅ Accounting Complete
No pending CCA, agency objections, or claims
⏱️ Time Elapsed: 4 Years
Target: Reduce to 3 years by decoupling accounting
"With the improvement made, accounting processes can be excluded. Pop-up in system allows selecting which AWB continues in accounting."
— Solution Team Meeting

MESSAGING ARCHITECTURE
📨 INTEGRATION LANDSCAPE
SITA
Airline ↔ GHA communication. Traditional telex-based.
CHAMP (PIMA)
Agent ↔ Airline communication. Per-message fee.
TGC (Traxon Global Customs)
Customs declarations for EU + other regions.
One Record (Future)
Blockchain-like shared platform. Eliminates CHAMP fees. Pilot with Basware ongoing.
🚀 ONE RECORD BENEFITS
• Eliminate per-message CHAMP fees
• Real-time data sharing with all parties
• Will become IATA mandatory
