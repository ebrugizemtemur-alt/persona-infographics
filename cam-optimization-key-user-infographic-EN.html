⚡ FLIGHT OPTIMIZATION SPECIALIST PERSONA

CAM - Capacity Management Module | Revenue Optimization Team Key User Profile

"Her uçuştan maksimum geliri sağlamak için kapasite ve booking'leri optimize ediyoruz"
1
PERSONA SNAPSHOT
⚡
Flight Optimization Specialist
Revenue Optimization / Uçuş Optimizasyon Şefliği
PRIMARY GOAL
Ensure aircraft carries optimal load by managing capacity, resolving TBA flights, and reassigning bookings for maximum revenue
KEY USERS
Kerem Hazım Aydemir, Onur İskender, Ezgi Karatepe (Flight Optimization Chef)
DEPARTMENT
Kargo Gelir Optimizasyon Müdürlüğü - Uçuş Optimizasyon Şefliği
CAPACITY TYPES MANAGED
⚖️ Weight-Based 📦 Volume-Based 📍 Position-Based
2
JOBS TO BE DONE
🔄 TBA Flight Resolution
Identify flights in TBA status (To Be Advised) due to capacity overflow or restrictions. Investigate root cause via Flight History and resolve by capacity adjustment or booking reassignment.
✈️ Booking Reassignment
Move bookings between flights when capacity issues arise. Select alternative flights, capture irregularity reasons, handle spot booking cancellations, and check embargo lists.
📊 Segment Capacity Management
Define and adjust segment-level capacities. Handle multi-leg flights where each segment may have different capacity constraints.
🔒 Flight Control & Access
Control flight booking access by station. Allow/restrict specific stations from booking on open/closed flights.
📈 Last-Minute Capacity Optimization
Monitor and adjust capacity close to departure. Check OPR010 for ready-to-load shipments that can be moved to earlier flights.

PAIN POINTS MATRIX
Pain Point Description Business Impact Severity
TBA Root Cause Investigation When flight goes TBA, must navigate to SHR011 via FLT002 to find root cause from logs/messages Time-consuming investigation delays resolution; flights stay in limbo CRITICAL
Multi-Step Reassignment Process Reassigning booking requires: Select flight → Capture Irregularity → Handle Spot Booking warning → Check Embargo Complex workflow increases error risk and processing time HIGH
Segment vs Flight Capacity Confusion Multi-leg flights show same total capacity for all legs; must check each segment separately Misleading capacity view can cause overbooking or underutilization HIGH
Physical vs Commercial Capacity Gap Discrepancy between physical aircraft capacity and commercially available capacity causes confusion Difficult to explain to sales teams; revenue optimization decisions affected MEDIUM
Netline Data Sync Issues Must verify flight info matches between Netline and COMIS; discrepancies require manual correction Data inconsistency leads to operational errors MEDIUM
5
TBA RESOLUTION WORKFLOW
1
Detect TBA Flight
Flight enters TBA status due to restriction or capacity overflow - visible in CAP147 Affected List
2
Identify TBA Reason
Check CAP147 → View TBA reasons next to bookings in Affected List tab
3
Investigate Root Cause
FLT002 → Flight History → SHR011 to see all logs and messages
4
Resolve: Capacity Increase
If capacity overflow: temporarily increase segment capacity via CAP145
5
Resolve: Booking Reassignment
CAP018 → Reassign → Select Flight (CAP149) → Capture Irregularity → Check Embargo
6
Reactivate Flight
FLT002 → Select flight → Revalidate to make flight active again
