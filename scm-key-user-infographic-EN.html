👤 Persona Snapshot

📍 Who They Are

Special Cargo and Healthcare Products Department team members (Başak, Durukan, Yusuf, Enes) managing high-value, sensitive cargo that requires specialized handling, documentation, and regulatory compliance.

🎯 Primary Goal

Ensure safe, compliant transportation of special cargo categories including pharmaceuticals, live animals, dangerous goods, radioactive materials, and valuable cargo while maximizing revenue from this premium segment.

⚡ Key Trigger

Booking requests for special cargo requiring consultancy on packaging, labeling, permits, and handling requirements. Each product category has unique processes that differ from standard cargo.

📊 Success Metrics

Zero compliance violations, accurate documentation, proper handling of ~1000 daily special cargo bookings (mostly via Istanbul), and maintaining ~50% revenue contribution from special cargo products.

✅ Jobs to be Done

📋 Product Consultancy & Guidance

Provide expert consultancy on special cargo requirements. Guide agents and customers on packaging, labeling, documentation, and container requirements per IATA manuals.

⚠️ Dangerous Goods Compliance

Ensure DG transportation follows international airline regulations and IATA rules. Update DG list annually from IATA and upload to COMIS. Identify misdeclared cargo.

🐾 Live Animal Handling

Manage live animal transportation with veterinary checks. Enforce special rules (e.g., day-old chicks cannot share aircraft with other producers). Block endangered species.

💊 Pharmaceutical Standards

Enforce company temperature and handling standards for pharma products. Reject non-compliant agent requests even if they want different conditions.

📜 Permit Management

Coordinate special permits required in certain countries for special cargo. Ensure all regulatory requirements are met before shipment.

🔍 Misdeclaration Detection

Work with dedicated teams to identify misdeclared cargo. Issue warnings to agents, maintain blacklist, and handle confirmed violation cases including cargo destruction.

📦 Special Cargo Product Categories

⚠️
Critical

Dangerous Goods (DG)

IATA regulated. Annual list updates. Packaging & labeling per manual. Includes chemicals like gold powder.

💊
Sensitive

Pharmaceuticals

Temperature-controlled. Company standards override agent requests. Healthcare products team.

🐾
Special

Live Animals (AVI)

Veterinary checks required. No endangered species. Day-old chicks have exclusive handling rules.

☢️
Critical

Radioactive Materials

Packaging varies by aircraft type. Strict containment requirements. Special handling protocols.

💎
Regulated

Valuable Cargo (VAL)

High-security handling. Some items overlap with DG (e.g., gold powder). Secure chain of custody.

⚰️
Special

Human Remains (HUM)

Separate handling process. Documentation requirements. Respectful transportation protocols.

😤 Pain Points & Challenges

Foreign Partner Challenges

High

Foreign partners face process challenges due to differences in local practices. Varying regulations across countries create inconsistencies.

Agent Non-Compliance Requests

High

Pharmaceutical agents request different temperature/handling conditions. Must enforce company standards despite customer pressure.

Annual DG List Updates

Medium

IATA updates DG list and Excel files annually. Manual upload to COMIS required. Risk of outdated information during transition.

Cross-Category Products

Medium

Some products span multiple categories (e.g., gold powder = DG + VAL). Complex classification and dual compliance requirements.
