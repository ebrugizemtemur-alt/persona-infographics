👤 Persona Snapshot

📍 Who They Are

Pricing team members (Yusuf, Gizem, Dilara) managing cargo tariffs, contracts, and promotional pricing. They work closely with Sales teams and Revenue Management to optimize pricing strategies across all products and routes.

🎯 Primary Goal

Define and maintain competitive pricing that maximizes revenue while meeting customer needs. Ensure all fee types (tariff, promo, contract, spot) are accurately configured in both COMIS and ACRM systems.

⚡ Key Trigger

Sales team requests for price changes, seasonal tariff updates (especially October-December), and spot pricing requests that require evaluation against MCR (Minimum Contributive Revenue) thresholds.

📊 Success Metrics

Spot approval turnaround time (target: reduce from 300 min avg), contract SLA compliance (5 business days), tariff accuracy, and integration consistency between ACRM-COMIS systems.

✅ Jobs to be Done

📋 Tariff Definition & Updates

Define tariffs based on product, SCC, handling code, commodity, O/D, and date ranges. Upload via Subrates → CSV → COMIS flow. Update pricing seasonally (avg. twice/year).

⚡ Spot Request Processing

Evaluate spot pricing requests from ACRM/COMIS/TK GO. Check against MCR/hurdle rate. Apply auto-approval rules (Red/Yellow/Green) or manual review within SLA.

📝 Contract Management

Process contract requests from Sales via ACRM (80%) or email (20%). Define fixed prices for key accounts. Manage BSA, allotment, and protocol agreements.

🎁 Promo Pricing

Define monthly promotional pricing (deadline: 20th of month). Promos override tariff prices when lower. Coordinate with Sales teams on campaign periods.

💱 Currency Management

Monitor exchange rate changes via Rate Tracking Robot (5% threshold alerts). Update fees when currency fluctuations exceed thresholds. Coordinate with Accounting on SHR054.

📊 MCR/Hurdle Rate Compliance

Ensure all prices meet MCR (MMR + Bid Price) requirements. Work with RM teams on dynamic pricing parameters. Validate that no fee falls below hurdle rate.

😤 Pain Points & Challenges

CCA Auto-Opening

High

Automatic CCA revision opening when reservations update. System doesn't recalculate prices properly, causing high volume of charge corrections.

ACRM-COMIS Desync

High

Initial reservation takes ACRM price, but updates revert to COMIS price. Changes from non-reservation screens cause unexpected price changes.

Excel → CSV → Subrates Manual Flow

High

Multi-step manual process for fee uploads. Subrates → SFTP → COMIS flow is error-prone. Duplicate fee lines can occur in live environment.

Date Manipulation Exploit

High

Users can enter past shipping dates to get historical lower prices. COMIS allows this while ACRM correctly blocks promo exploitation.

1-Hour Sync Delay

Medium

ACRM pulls from COMIS hourly. Reservations made in between may get old prices. Not fully synchronized, causing accepted losses.

Email-Based Requests

Medium

Promo, tariff, and adhoc prices still processed via email. No SLA tracking. Target is 100% ACRM but currently ~80% for contracts.
